About WBF

Wisdom Bharati Forum (WBF) is an educational initiative dedicated to holistic student development. WBF creates a platform where students can grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially through community-based learning and mentorship.

Established with a vision to transform education through community engagement, WBF facilitates theme-based student communities and offers various programs designed to enhance academic excellence, personal growth, and professional readiness.

Mission & Identity

Our Mission

To create vibrant learning communities that foster holistic development, critical thinking, and collaborative problem-solving among students.

Our Vision

A world where education transcends traditional boundaries, creating lifelong learners who contribute meaningfully to society.

Core Values

  • Community Learning - Fostering collaborative educational environments
  • Holistic Development - Nurturing intellectual, emotional, and social growth
  • Innovation - Encouraging creative approaches to education and problem-solving
  • Inclusivity - Ensuring educational opportunities for all

Organizational Structure

WBF operates through a structured framework of Theme-Based Student Communities and Core Offices that work together to create a comprehensive educational ecosystem.

Theme-Based Student Communities

  • Academic Excellence Groups
  • Research & Innovation Clusters
  • Leadership & Management Teams(Commerce Vaishali Guild )
  • Arts & Cultural Exploration(Humanities Bhaskara Circle )
  • Technology & Digital Literacy(Tech Visweshwarya Forum )

Core Offices ("Ministries")

  • OFCC – Office of Communication & Coordination
  • OSAW – Office of Student Advancement and welfare
  • RAW – Research & Analysis Wing
  • SSPA – Student Scam Protection and Awreness

WBFx

WBFx is WBF’s in-house execution arm (currently unincorporated), created to turn ideas into action. It operates as a youth execution engine working across:
AI & emerging tech
Sustainability and climate
Consulting for NGOs/startups
Project implementation-as-a-service

WBFx generates revenue through micro-consulting, knowledge products, and project delivery, while contributing a share back to WBF.

WBFx is expected to become a private limited company in FY 2025–26.

Student Journey

Onboarding

Introduction to WBF ecosystem, assessment of interests and strengths, and community placement.

Community Integration

Active participation in chosen communities, establishing peer connections, and setting personal development goals.

Skills Development

Targeted workshops, mentorship programs, and collaborative projects to build both hard and soft skills.

Leadership Growth

Taking on community responsibilities, leading initiatives, and mentoring newer members.

Professional Readiness

Industry connections, internship opportunities, and career guidance to prepare for post-academic success.

Community Sessions
